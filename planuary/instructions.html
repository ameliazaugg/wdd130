Home Tips and Tricks Instructions
  1. WHY: Why do you want to make a webcomic? Why do you want to make this webcomic in particular?
  2. ELEVATOR PITCH: Give us a quick premise for your comic!
  3. TAGLINE: What's a punchy phrase to summarize your comic?
  4. MAIN CHARACTER: Who is the comic about?
  5. COMPANION: Who is the main character's best friend?
  6. ENEMY: Who does the main character clash with?
  7. NEW CLOTHES: What sort of costume change do they go through?
  8. INVENTORY: What's in the main character's bag?
  9. PROP: What's an important item in your comic?
  10. MEME: What's a goofy joke you can make about your comic?
  11. THEME SONG: What song makes you think of your comic?
  12. MOODBOARD: What images and quotes have the perfect vibes for your comic?
  13. HOMETOWN: What's the broader setting for your comic?
  14. HQ: Where do the characters operate from?
  15. FAVORITE PLACE: Where does your main character love to go?
  16. TRIVIA: What are some fun facts about your characters?
  17. LIKES + DISLIKES: What do they like and dislike?
  18. DARK HORSE: What side character has stolen your heart?
  19. SHADOW: How do the characters look in a silhouette test?
  20. PALETTE: What colors would you love to use in your comic?
  21. STYLE: What are some notes on how you want your comic to look?
  22. INSIDE JOKE: What's a reference to your comic that no one will get yet?
  23. DYNAMIC: What's your favorite duo of characters?
  24. TEASER: What is one scene you can't get out of your head?
  25. INSPIRATION: What gave you the idea for this comic?
  26. ANTHEM: What do you listen to while drawing?
  27. RESCUE PHRASE: What's a quote to motivate you when things get tough?
  28. TITLE: What's the title for your comic?
  29. COVER: Put all of the previous prompts together to make a cover for your comic!
  30. REFLECTION: Remake one of the previous prompts. Did your answer to the prompt change?
  31. CELEBRATE: You made it! Tell us how you feel about your comic now that you spent a month working on it.
