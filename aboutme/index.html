All About Amy

Amy is Quite the Quirky Character!

I'm a 20-something-year-old pursuing a degree in Software Development through BYU Pathway Worldwide. When I'm not puzzling over the arcane arts of HTML and Python, I like drawing cutesy cartoon bugs, writing fiction about finding hope in hard times, and making comics about all sorts of things-- everything from the miracles of daily life to adventures set in imaginary worlds.

While I'm only a student at the moment, I plan to become an application developer for my career. I also plan to apply the knowledge I gain over the course of my education to make simple, wholesome video games that pass on important messages rooted in Gospel truth.
